Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  4. Drain the pasta and toss it with the garlic and tomato mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and top with basil and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
